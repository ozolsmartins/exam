Oz

Professional beatmaker

Martins Ozols (born April 14, 2004) is a Latvian record producer from Tukums, Latvia, who is part of the collective Fanaats label. He gained notoriety for his work with late rapper Lil Miscenkovs and has been attributed for the development of Lil Miscenkovs's signature sound. Some of Ozols's most well known work includes rapper Lil Miscenkovs's tracks "Jauntukuma strelka," "Durbes lyfe," and "Fanaatu dziive," along with rapper Kurtish's (Carti) "Carti adlibs" and the Oz's single "Nice." Ozols's work consists primarily of hip-hop instrumentals, some rap/trap instrumentals and he has contributed production to the Fanaati collective album TK (2020).
